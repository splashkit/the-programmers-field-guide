Our first trek will explore the central role of sequence in our programs.

At the end of this trek, make sure you can:

- Create small programs that:

  - Contain a sequence of instructions that achieve something
  - Use the SplashKit library
  - Call procedures, with a range of arguments

- Correctly use programming terminology to explain your code.
